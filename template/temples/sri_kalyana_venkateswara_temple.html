Sri Kalyana Venkateswara Temple

A sacred temple where Lord Venkateswara stayed after marriage

This temple is located 12 km from Tirupati. It is believed that Lord Venkateswara stayed here for six months after his marriage before moving to Tirumala. The temple is a significant pilgrimage site for devotees.
