Govindaraja Temple

An ancient Vaishnavite temple in the heart of Tirupati

Sri Govindarajaswamy Temple is an ancient Hindu Vaishnavite temple situated in the heart of Tirupati city in Tirupati district of Andhra Pradesh in India. It is one of the most important temples in the region, known for its historical and spiritual significance.
