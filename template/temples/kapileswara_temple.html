Kapileswara Temple

A sacred temple dedicated to Lord Shiva in Tirupati

Kapila Theertham is the holy waterfall in Tirupati named after Kapila Muni. It hosts the Kapileswara Temple, which is dedicated to Lord Shiva. The deity is referred to as Kapileswara, and the temple is a significant pilgrimage site for devotees.
